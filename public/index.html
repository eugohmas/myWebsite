Timeline

1992-2008: The Early Years

I was born in 1992 which makes me a crazy millenial, if I am not mistaken. I was born in Connecticut where I lived until 2008 with my family. During my childhood, I loved to take things apart to figure out how they worked, although, at that time, I struggled to put them back together. I also loved to be outdoors and on the computer. I am the third of 4 kids, three of which are boys and one girl. I played soccer and hockey up until highschool. Then I decided to just play hockey in highschool. In middle school and highschool, I found myself both excelling and enjoying math and science because I had a drive to problem solve. This ultimately led me to choose electrical engineering for a college major.

2008-2010: The End of an Era

In 2008, my family moved to Bozeman, Montana. I finished my highschool years in Montana. I enjoyed skiing, hiking, mountain biking, and camping as much as I could during these years. My parents also enjoyed these activities so we got to do them as a family as well. I worked construction , as well as at a subway and a taco time during this period.
